On the importance of Self-confidence（个人成长）

As we all know, the self-confidence plays an indispensable role in one’s personal achievement. The absence of assertiveness will definitely lead to one’s lower achievement and inadequate personal growth, which is beyond any dispute.
From my personal perspective, if there were no self_confidence we would never taste the real happiness of success. Dr. Zhong Nanshan, the giant of the medical field and the great hero of our age, severs as the shining and fitting example here. The assertiveness can be regarded as the fundamental factor for his glorious success, according to an interview of him. He attributes his rational decision and sound judgement to his self_confidence. There is no stretch to say that without confidence he can not give the critical suggestion at the decisive moment and save millions of lives.
Elon Musk, the founder of Space-X and Tesla, a great innovator of our age, serves as a shining and fitting example here. The self-confidence is the critical element for his glorious success. It is self-confidence that helps him create enormous values to the general public and fulfill the great personal ambition.
At the end of day, one’s success lies in the spirit of self-confidence. There is no exaggeration to say that the confidence is the part and parcel that forge the supporting structure of one’s personal growth. If we are confident enough and put adequate efforts to our study or work, the success will not be far away from us.

社会（科技）话题

智能手机

The topic concerning the overuse of smart devices has captured wide media attention, occupying newspaper headlines and sparking heated discussion on the web. The smart device is and remains to be an indispensable part for the sound growth of our society.
From my personal perspective, it is not hard to imagine that if the current state of affairs about the information overload is to continue as it is, we would pay the high price. The limitation of overuse of smart devices is long overdue. Not surprisingly, this topic makes good copy in the newspaper and touches the deep cord of social progress, which is a good start.
As the college students, we are expected to use mobile phone reasonably and wisely. The wise use of mobile Internet should be known and followed by people in every profession and in every corner. And once rational use of social media becomes the common practice that happens on a daily basis, our world will be a better homeland. It is true that there is a long way for us to go before we reach our ideal. Nevertheless, the coordinated mechanism and shared efforts will be the best guarantee.

The preservation of our environment is long overdue. Not surprisingly, this topic makes good copy in the newspaper and touches the deep cord of social progress, which is a good start.

How to develop good communication skills

The topic of how to develop good communication skills has captured our attention, being social media trending topic and sparking heated discussion among us. The interpersonal communication skills do put us in a favorable position in the job market. Jack Ma is a good example here.
Firstly, the communication skills demand our self-confidence. The self-confidence plays an indispensable role in our interpersonal relations. The absence of self-confidence will definitely lead to one’s bad communication. So, being confident is the foremost. Secondly, the honesty underlies good communication. There is no exaggeration to say that the honesty is the steel that forges the supporting structure of good communication. Therefore, being honesty is always the best policy. Last but not the least, the good reading habit provides us with more opportunities to share ideas with others. To be sure, the good communication lies in one’s exchange of opinions. Then, reading more will help us a lot.
It is true that there are various approaches to develop the skills of communication, I firmly believe. The attention to this topic is a good start. More rational and practical methods can be generated by our discussion. However, finding your own way always pays.
